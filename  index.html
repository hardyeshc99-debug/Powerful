5-minute power reset

For the moments when the job feels heavy and there is nobody beside you in the team.

1
Breathe like you are safe.Inhale for 4, hold for 2, exhale for 6. Do it three times. Your nervous system listens.
2
Drink water and stand up.Move your body for 60 seconds. Stretch your neck, wrists, back, and jaw.
3
Pick only the next task.Not the entire mountain. Just the next stone. Write it down and finish that one thing.
4
Tell yourself the truth.“This is hard, but I am capable. I have handled hard days before.”
5
Celebrate a tiny win.Send yourself a mental high-five. Progress counts even when it is invisible to others.
